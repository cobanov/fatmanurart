Fatmanur

A contemporary artist exploring the intersection of emotion and form through bold brushstrokes and vibrant color palettes.

Her work draws inspiration from the complexity of human experience, translating raw emotion into visual narratives that resonate with viewers on a deeply personal level.

Based in Istanbul, Fatmanur has exhibited her work in galleries across Europe and continues to push the boundaries of traditional painting techniques.
