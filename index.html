Fatmanur Savruk

Professional Artist | Edirne, Turkey

Fatmanur Savruk is a distinguished painter based in the historic city of Edirne, known for her masterful oil paintings that capture the essence of Turkish architectural heritage and landscape. A graduate of Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, she has developed a distinctive style characterized by luminous glazing techniques and meticulous attention to atmospheric effects.

Working from her riverside studio in a restored 19th-century Ottoman house, Savruk has held seventeen solo exhibitions and participated in numerous international group shows across Turkey, Bulgaria, and Greece. Her acclaimed series "Bridges of Memory" was acquired by the Turkish Ministry of Culture and Tourism, establishing her reputation as one of Turkey's most promising contemporary artists.

In 2018, she received the Edirne Cultural Heritage Prize for her contributions to preserving and celebrating the city's artistic legacy through her art.
